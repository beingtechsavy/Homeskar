    Frequently asked questions

    How do Adobe Stock subscriptions work?

    Subscriptions give you the best deal on the price of our standard assets, which include royalty-free, high-resolution photos, vectors, illustrations, templates, 3D and audio.

    Most subscriptions also include HD videos and a 20% discount on purchases of 4K video, extended licenses, editorial and Premium.

    Subscriptions are monthly or annual, with annual commitment plans providing higher discounts per asset.

    How does the Adobe Stock free trial work?

    The Adobe stock free trial allows you to download assets included in your plan for free during your trial.

    You can cancel risk-free during your trial without being charged. Any assets downloaded during the free trial period are yours to keep, even if you cancel.

    After your trial ends, your annual subscription begins and you will automatically be charged the rate listed in your plan.

    What types of stock assets are included in a subscription?

    Subscriptions plans provide access to royalty-free, high-resolution standard images, as well as standard templates, 3D assets, and music tracks.

    Most plans also provide access to HD video. Plans with video also provide a 20% discount on purchases of 4K video, extended licenses, and premium.

    What type of license do I get with subscription downloads?

    Most assets come with a standard license. You may purchase extended licenses with some subscriptions at a 20% discount.

    You can find more information about license types, including extended licenses right here

    What happens to my unused subscription licenses? What is a "rollover"?

    The 2,394.22/mo plans and the 6,386.16/mo and 7,982.70/mo plans include rollover benefits.

    You can rollover unused licenses or subscription credits for future use as long as you have your subscription. Rollover amount varies by plan.

    If you cancel your subscription or downgrade your plan to a lower priced plan, you’ll lose any unused licenses or plan credits.

    What happens if I need more licenses than I'm allotted by my subscription?

    Your subscription allows you to pay on-demand for additional assets at the same discount available in your plan.

    What happens if I cancel my subscription?

    If you cancel your subscription, you'll lose access to the Adobe Stock service and forfeit your unused licenses and plan credits.

    How can I download assets not available with subscription?

    Assets not available in subscription plans, such as videos and Premium collection, are available individually for download on-demand at the listed prices on the site.

    You can also use Credit Packs, which provide discounted savings across multiple assets.

    What's an extended license?

    Standard licenses allow you to use the licensed image perpetually, but your print runs can't exceed 500,000 and you can't use the image in any items you're going to sell. For example, you can't print the image on a coffee mug to sell. The print run limit doesn't apply when the image is only for display on websites, social media, or mobile applications.

    If you need large print runs or you're using the asset in resale, you need to purchase an extended license.

    What can I use my credits for?

    Credit packs can be used to license any asset.

    They last for one year (6 months only in Japan, if you have purchased as an individual). You can buy as many as you need, and the larger the credit pack, the less each credit costs. Click here for detailed terms.

    Do credits expire?

    If you reside outside of Japan, unused credits in your credit pack automatically expire 12 months from the date of purchase. If you are a resident of Japan, unused credits automatically expire 6 months from the date of purchase.

    Should I purchase a subscription plan or a credit pack?

    Subscriptions are best if you have ongoing stock content needs. Most of our plans provide access to our entire collection at a discount. Our plans range from up to 3 to 750 standard assets a month.

    Credit packs are designed for those who have fluctuating stock content needs.

    You may purchase both credit packs and subscription plans individually or buy both.

    Credits from credit packs and credits from subscription plans are not interchangeable so cannot be combined to license assets.

    May I share my license with others?

    If you purchase a team subscription plan, you may share assets with the members of your team. Check out our Teams page for details.

    Do I need to use other Adobe products in order to use Adobe Stock?

    No, you don't need any other Adobe product to use Adobe Stock. However, there are a number of benefits to using Adobe Stock with other Adobe Creative Cloud products, such as the ability to preview, edit and license in Adobe Photoshop before completing your purchase.
